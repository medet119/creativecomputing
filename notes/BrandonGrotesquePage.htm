Brandon
Grotesque

A functional geometric sans with a warm touch

a font by Hannes von Döhren (2010)

Brandon Grotesque is influenced by the geometric-style sans serif faces that were popular during the 1920s and 30s, the fonts are based on geometric forms that have been optically corrected for better legibility. The small x-height and the restrained forms lend it a distinctive elegance. The Brandon Grotesque weights are great performers in display sizes. For longer texts and small sizes we created the perfectly fitting Brandon Text Family.
